ABOUT THE COMPANY

Indonature was founded in 2009. Headquartered in Lombok, NTB. At the beginning of our establishment, we produced dried botanical decoration and then developed and had two additional products: wooden decorations and furniture.

Indonature is a privately owned company with grown and has two main locations in Lamongan, East Java and Lombok, NTB. Indonature helps empower local artisans to continue to produce the highest quality original Indonesian art.

With over ten years of experience in shipping at home and abroad, our team always tries to produce wooden handicrafts by prioritizing quality and customer satisfaction.
